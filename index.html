About Us

As two curious Kazakh teenagers, we created Aqyryn Soile to explore the topics that are often overlooked in our society. We believe that by discussing these issues openly, we can learn from each other and broaden our perspectives.

Our podcast covers a range of topics that are not commonly talked about in Kazakhstan, including mental health, environmentalism, and LGBTQ+ rights. We invite guests from diverse backgrounds to share their experiences and insights with our audience, and we also provide a platform for our listeners to share their own stories.

Through Aqyryn Soile, we hope to inspire others to engage in meaningful conversations and embrace new ideas. Join us as we explore the uncharted territory of the human experience, one episode at a time.
